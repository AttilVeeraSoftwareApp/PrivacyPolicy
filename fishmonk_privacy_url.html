About us

FISH MONK is a food delivery self-service in which a FISH MONK food-delivery company delivers food to a customer to avoid longer wait time.
An order is typically made either through a FISH MONK mobile app.
The delivered items can include different varieties of FISH FOODS.

Customers can, depending on the delivery company, choose to pay online or in person, with cash or card or UPI Payment.
A flat rate delivery fee is often charged with what the customer has bought.
Sometimes no delivery fees are charged depending upon the situation.
[1] Tips are often customary for food delivery service.
[2] Contactless delivery as it is a self-service system.

Other aspects of food delivery include catering and wholesale food service deliveries to restaurants, cafeterias, health care facilities,
and caterers by foodservice distributors.

Services Provided by FISH MONK

FISH MONK Use Pre-Orders technique. It enable customers to make a food order reservation ahead of time. Customers place the order, and then arrive promptly to avoid waiting in queue to collect food. This food collection reservation system can be taken to a whole new level. Allow customers to pre-order their meals by using an online android application "FISH MONK", so that customers can order their food, stroll in and pick it up. That way, moments later their food is on the table and still piping hot. Doing so allows the kitchen to organize more efficiently and moves customers along their journey in a smooth process.
